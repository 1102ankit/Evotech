Guidelines

  1. Each team can have maximum 4 members and minimum 2 members
  2. Teams have to bring all the software and hardware components required by them to pitch up the project themselves.
  3. Every team would be given 2 hours to set up their project and 3 hours for the exhibition.
  4. Teams have to build a working prototype for exhibition otherwise they will not be judged in the competition.
  5. At the end of the exhibition each team would be given 10 mins for their demonstration to the judges.

Eligibility Criteria

  1. Students of all undergraduate and post graduate courses from Delhi-NCR colleges are welcome to participate in this event.
  2. All the team members have to be from same college.
  3. Students can present projects from any discipline of software and hardware.
  4. There is no restriction on the genre of the projects.

How to Register

  1. Teams have to register themselves online at http://evotechbvp.tech/
  2. All teams have to build their project before the competition/ exhibition and submit a short presentation while registering online for the event. The presentation should not exceed 8 slides.
  3. Format for the presentation is as follows :

    • Title (Project name and College name)
    • Introduction (Problem and Solution)
    • Technologies involved
    • Competitive analysis to similar existing technology
    • Feasibility and Applications

2. Exhibition and Competition (Offline)

* Both Software and Hardware projects are allowed.
